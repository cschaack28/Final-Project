Why I Train

Training is the easiest way for me to stay consistent. If I’m doing the basics (sleep, food, lifting), everything else feels easier to manage.

My Training Priorities

  1. Recovery
    • Sleep 7–9 hours
    • Stretch / mobility
    • Deload when needed
  2. Nutrition
    • Protein first
    • Hydrate
    • Keep it consistent
  3. Progress
    • Track lifts
    • Add weight or reps
    • Keep form clean
