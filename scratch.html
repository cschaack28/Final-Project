Why Training Matters

Training isn’t just about lifting more weight. It’s about stacking small wins, showing up when you don’t feel like it, and making consistency a non-negotiable part of your identity.

Training Priorities

To keep training sustainable, I try to organize my focus into a few practical layers:

  1. Foundations
    • Sleep at least 7–8 hours consistently.
    • Hit reasonable protein and total calories for the week.
    • Build a schedule that actually fits real life.
  2. Execution
    • Track workouts with simple notes or a basic log.
    • Progress weights or reps gradually over time.
    • Prioritize control and form over ego lifting.
  3. Reflection
    • Review progress every few weeks instead of every day.
    • Adjust training blocks when school, work, or life shifts.
    • Celebrate consistency more than one-off PR numbers.
